Our Purpose

CodeRisk AI is engineered to bridge the gap between Rapid Development and Robust Security. By utilizing machine learning patterns and cyclomatic complexity metrics, we predict high-risk code segments before they enter the production pipeline, effectively neutralizing zero-day vulnerabilities at the logic layer.
